# 1

Good evening Twingkey, my goddess!!
大家好!

Goddess Twingkey approached her next target city at night.
The army and air force attacked her with their full power, but they could not stop her.
Twingkey overlooked the whole city.

"Wow, this is a bigger one. ^^"
"There are many tall buildings and a big bridge!"
"... I am too small for this city. OK, I become bigger again!, 呵呵."

So, she focused the crystal power, and release it!
She became bigger! Ah! It's giga size!!!

Giga size Twingkey crushed a lot of buildings at once with her shining white boots.
She also destroyed building one by one with her huge finger.
Ah!! Now her finger is much bigger than a building!!
