"Heather's Ant" - the story is about Heather's boyfriend who was shrinking little by little until he was now ant size, the story began here, when he is so small and his girlfriend Heather is tired to take care of someone so small, that he decided to keep him like a micro-pet if he acomplish some little games for her.

“希瑟的螞蟻” - 這個故事是關於希瑟的男朋友是誰一點點縮小，直到我現在螞蟻大小，故事從這裡開始的，當時他是那麼小，他的女友希瑟累了照顧別人這麼小，有決定保留他像一個微型寵物，如果我完成一些小遊戲，為她。
