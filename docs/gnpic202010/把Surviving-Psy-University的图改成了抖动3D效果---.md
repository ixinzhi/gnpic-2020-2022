# 1

Surviving Psy University，一个第一人称冒险漫画，在这可以下载原版： [http://jeffos.deviantart.com](http://jeffos.deviantart.com/)

不过原版是对眼3D的，对于不会看对眼3D图的实在是一种折磨...于是自己把这改成了抖动3D的...

原理很简单，左眼右眼两张图分别作为两帧，然后在AE里调节时间伸缩，然后打开AE的帧融合，AE会自动生成中间帧的内容..

由于是自动生成的，画面有些部分会有点撕裂..不过...不要在意这些细节了..至少3D效果有了..

本来想翻译一下再发的..翻译了一小段后发现自己的英语水平对于这种估计达到六级难度的句子还是有些吃力...还是放弃了..

好了..不多说，开始放图..顺便求奖励点...

序幕

The year is 21XX. Electronic technology has stagnated. However, biological research has not. Humans

have unlocked the ability to tap into psychic, or psionic powers after decades of research.   At the

same time, humanity began to enter a decline, the world descending into violence. The Internet as it

was in the 21

st

century collapsed, leading to the countries closing themselves off from each other, wars

to brew. The new weapons they used were psionics, but unlike technology, they weren't simply

devised and built, but instead gained through individual work and stress. Once peaceful universities,

now devoted to harness psychic potential, became the graveyards for prospective students in psionics

who didn't make the cut.

The protagonist of the story is you, otherwise known as John Meecro, a student of the seemingly

peaceful but actually dangerous Western American Psy University. You're a young adult who was

fortunate to get a scholarship to this school, and you've spend the past semester and a half there

studying, training.

Those who do not not make the cut in terms of unlocking their psychic ability and their pursuit of the

education find themselves expelled from the school,which accounts for nearly 35% of the freshman

class each semester.   Even then, not even students who discover their psionic talents make the cut,

encouraging infighting. While murder isn't technically legal here, disappearances with no confessing

witnesses aren't investigated, either, and many students 'disappear' over the course of the school year.

People appear civil in public and in class, but when no one else is looking...things happen.

You are one of those near the edge of failing, one who hasn't even unlocked his psychic ability yet, and

thus has been shielded from much of the dangerous parts of school...until now.
